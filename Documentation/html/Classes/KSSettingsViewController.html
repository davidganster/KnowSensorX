Called when any of the sliders (minimumIdleTimeSlider or screenshotQualitySlider) change their value. The minimumIdleTimeSlider is continuous and updates the minimum idle time in KSUserInfo. The screenshotQualitySlider is discrete and directly passes its value to KSUserInfo.
